Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. I've worked with companies at every stage—from pre-launch startups to public enterprises. The key is having a clear vision and commitment to exceptional user experience.

What's your typical project timeline?

Most projects range from 4-16 weeks depending on scope. Design systems and enterprise work tend toward the longer end. I'll provide a detailed timeline in your proposal.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. I offer post-launch optimization reviews and monthly retainer options for continued partnership.

Can you work with our existing development team?

Definitely. I collaborate closely with in-house teams, providing detailed specs, assets, and implementation support. I've worked with teams using React, Angular, Vue, and various other frameworks.

What if we're not sure what we need?

That's exactly why the discovery phase exists. We'll start with a conversation about your goals and challenges, and I'll recommend the right approach.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, I'm happy to sign NDAs and work within your security requirements.
