What it measures

Your website has one job: turn the people who find you into customers who call, book, or walk in the door. The UX Health Check looks at the five things that most often get in the way — explained in plain English, no jargon.

First Impression
Whether a first-time visitor instantly understands what your business does and how to reach you — before they click over to a competitor.
Conversion
How well your site turns visitors into calls, bookings, or form submissions — instead of clicks that go nowhere.
Mobile
Most people will find you on their phone while out and about. Whether your site is easy to read, tap, and use on a small screen.
Accessibility
Whether everyone — including older visitors and people with limited vision — can read and use your site without trouble. It also widens the customers you can serve.
Consistency
Whether your site looks consistent and polished from page to page, so you come across as credible and trustworthy.
