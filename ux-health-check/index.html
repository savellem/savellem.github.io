What it measures

Your website has one job: turn the people who find you into customers. The UX Health Check looks at the five things that most often get in the way — explained in plain English, no jargon.

Onboarding
Whether a first-time visitor instantly understands what you offer and how to take the next step — before they lose interest and leave.
Conversion
How well your site turns visitors into real actions — sign-ups, purchases, or inquiries — instead of clicks that go nowhere.
Mobile
Most people will visit on their phone. Whether your site is easy to read, tap, and use on a small screen.
Accessibility
Whether everyone — including older visitors and people with limited vision — can read and use your site without trouble. It also widens your audience.
Design System
Whether your site looks consistent and polished from page to page, so you come across as credible and trustworthy.
