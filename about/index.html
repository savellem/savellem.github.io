Designer. Builder. Desert dweller.

My superpower is a mix of intuition and ideas that seem obvious to me but apparently aren't obvious to everyone else. I've been taking things apart and putting them back together differently since I was a kid. Remote controlled cars, mostly. In high school I taught myself HTML and CSS in the school library and helped redesign sections of the school website. Nobody asked me to. I just wanted to see if I could.

Four decades of that same curiosity later and it's just how I think. Pattern recognition, creative problem solving, and a stubborn belief that most things can be done better. Those aren't skills I developed, they're just how I'm wired.

I'm based in Scottsdale with my bull terrier Diego, who makes life considerably stranger. I've spent the last decade practicing tarot as a genuine decision-making framework, which sounds unusual until you realize it's really just a structured way to slow down and trust your instincts. I even built my own app for it. I also run a YouTube channel with about 50K subscribers built on gaming and music, and somewhere in the long-term plan is either retiring to make music full time or opening a karaoke bar. Whichever comes first.
