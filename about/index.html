About Me

Digital Experience Leader with 18+ Years

I'm a digital experience architect specializing in transforming business objectives into exceptional user experiences. Based in Scottsdale, Arizona, I leverage AI-powered tools and modern frameworks (React, Angular, component-based architectures) to accelerate product development while maintaining design excellence.

My expertise encompasses AI-enhanced design workflows, atomic design systems, data-driven UX research, A/B testing, digital marketing strategy, and cross-functional team leadership. I combine traditional UX principles with cutting-edge solutions—using AI for rapid prototyping and intelligent automation alongside contemporary frameworks—always in service of meaningful business outcomes and delightful user experiences.
